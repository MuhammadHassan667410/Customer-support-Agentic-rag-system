NOVATECH SOLUTIONS — PRICING PLANS
====================================

OVERVIEW
--------
NovaTech Solutions offers three pricing plans designed to fit teams at every stage — from independent freelancers and small startups to large enterprises with complex requirements. All plans include access to the NovaTech web platform, desktop app, and mobile app (iOS and Android).

Pricing is per workspace, not per user on the Starter plan, and per user per month on Professional and Enterprise plans. All prices are listed in USD.

---

STARTER PLAN — FREE FOREVER
-----------------------------
Price: $0/month
User limit: Up to 5 users per workspace
Projects: Up to 10 active projects
Storage: 5 GB total

The Starter plan is free forever — no credit card required and no trial expiration. It is designed for small teams, freelancers, and individuals who want to experience the core NovaTech platform without any financial commitment.

What is included in the Starter plan:
- Unlimited tasks and subtasks across all active projects
- List view, Board view, and Calendar view for tasks
- Team chat with up to 10 channels
- File attachments up to 25 MB per file
- Basic time tracking (manual log only)
- 3 built-in report templates (project progress, weekly summary, team tasks)
- Mobile app (iOS and Android)
- Email support with response time within 3 business days
- 2-factor authentication
- Up to 3 integrations (Slack, Google Calendar, and one of your choice)

Limitations of the Starter plan:
- Maximum 5 users (cannot add a 6th user without upgrading)
- Maximum 10 active projects at a time (archive to free slots)
- No Timeline (Gantt) view
- No Table view
- No API access
- No custom fields
- No audit logs
- No priority or live chat support
- No custom reports
- No scheduled report emails
- No webhooks
- No SSO or IP allowlisting
- No EU data residency

The Starter plan is ideal for: freelancers managing personal projects, small founding teams (2-5 people) validating a business idea, nonprofit organizations with limited budgets, students and educators.

FREQUENTLY ASKED PRICING QUESTIONS
------------------------------------

Q: Can I switch between plans at any time?
A: Yes. You can upgrade from Starter to Professional at any time and the change takes effect immediately. Downgrading from Professional to Starter is possible at the end of your current billing period. Upgrading from Professional to Enterprise requires contacting sales.

Q: What happens if my team exceeds 5 users on the Starter plan?
A: You will be prompted to upgrade to the Professional plan. You cannot add a 6th user to a Starter workspace — the system will block the invitation until you upgrade.

Q: Is there a free trial of the Professional plan?
A: Yes. New workspaces can try the Professional plan free for 14 days with no credit card required. At the end of the trial, you can choose to subscribe or revert to the Starter plan. No data is lost when reverting.

Q: Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or educational institutions?
A: Yes. Registered 501(c)(3) nonprofits and accredited educational institutions receive a 50% discount on the Professional plan. Contact sales@novatechsolutions.com with proof of eligibility.

Q: Do you offer discounts for startups?
A: NovaTech participates in several startup programs. If your startup is part of Y Combinator, Techstars, or any other recognized accelerator, contact us for startup pricing.

Q: How does billing work for mid-month upgrades?
A: If you upgrade mid-month, you are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing period. Your new billing cycle starts on your original billing date at the new plan rate.

Q: Can I add users without upgrading to a higher tier?
A: On the Starter plan, no — the 5-user limit is hard. On the Professional plan, you can add users at any time and you will be billed for the additional seats on a prorated basis.

Q: Are guest users included in the user count?
A: On the Professional and Enterprise plans, guest users (read-only or limited access) do not count toward your billable seat count. On the Starter plan, all users including guests count toward the 5-user limit.

Q: What currencies do you accept?
A: All plans are billed in USD. Enterprise customers can request invoicing in GBP or EUR.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover credit/debit cards via Stripe. Enterprise customers can pay by bank transfer (ACH or SWIFT wire). We do not currently accept PayPal.
